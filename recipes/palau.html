Description

In Kazakh cuisine kuurdak (braised meat and potatoes) is made from sheep's liver, kidney, heart and lungs.

Ingredients

  • 0.3 kg rice
  • 0.5 kg beef meat
  • 0.5 liters of water
  • 3 carrots
  • 2 onions

Steps

  1. Fry the meat.
  2. Add rice
  3. Simmer for 40 minutes
  4. Place on a plate
