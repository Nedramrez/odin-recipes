Kuurdak

Description

In Kazakh cuisine kuurdak (braised meat and potatoes) is made from sheep's liver, kidney, heart and lungs.

Ingredients

  • 0.2 kg sheep's liver
  • 0.2 kg sheep's kidney
  • 0.2 kg sheep's heart
  • 0.2 kg sheep's lungs
  • 5 potatoes
  • 2 sliced onion

Steps

  1. Fry the meat.
  2. Add onions and potatoes
  3. Place on a plate
