Beshbarmak

Description

Beshbarmak (Boiled meat with squares of noodles) is a dish from Central Asian cuisine. Beshbarmak basically means “five fingers” in Kazakhstan. It is probably the most popular dish in this country.

Ingredients

  • 2 kg horse meat
  • 1 sliced onion
  • 5 liters of water
  • Lasagna noodles
  • 1 spoon of salt

Steps

  1. Put the meat into cold water and boil it.
  2. Cook for 2,5 hours.
  3. After the meat is ready, remove it from and cook the noodles.
  4. Put into big plate noodles and meat.
